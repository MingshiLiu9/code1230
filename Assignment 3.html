Local education inequities across NSW

The issue in NSW

According to the information that we can get from the graph that the high educational level distribution is not equitable either male or female.

Latte line also illustrate the main reason why this phenomenon happened. Not only economic but also development cause educational equities across NSW.

The solution proposal

Case study

Because luddenham is approximately dark on the graph which means the education level is lower than other location in NSW, and the education level need to be improved. Therefore a university is a proper approach in order to improve the education level and provide more employment opportunities.

Design proposal

The shape of the university is circle such as a donut. The hole of the donut on the site is the lake. In addition that, the main body of the donut consists of five buildings which have different shape and height. The difference between each building is the shape and height which is subject to illustrate the spatial relationship. The design is from outside to inside, and the center is the lake. Enough space and connection between these buildings provide space to students for conversation and daily activities.
